# Operators in Solidity

# Arithmetic Operator
**Applicable on `integer` type operands**
## Addition: `+`
## Subtraction : `-`
## Multiplication: `*`
## Division : `/`
## Modulo (Modulus): `%`
## Exponential : ` ** `

# Logical Operator
**Applicable on `Boolean` type operands**
## Logical AND : `&&`
## Logical OR  : `||`
## Logical NOT : `!`

# Comparison Operator
**Applicable on `integer` type operands**
## Less than : `<`
## Less than or equal to : `<=`
## Greater than : `>`
## Greater than or equal to: `>=`
## Is equal to : `==`
## Not equal to : `!=`

# Bit Operator (or Bitwise)

## Bitwise AND : `&`
## Bitwise OR  : `|`
## Bitwise exclusive OR : `^`
## Bitwise NOT (negation): `~`
